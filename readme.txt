## About

This project represents my professional profile, featuring:

- **Welcome Section**: An engaging introduction to who I am.
- **About Section**: A detailed overview of my background and interests.
- **Skills Section**: Highlights of my technical and professional skills.
- **Experience Section**: A timeline showcasing my work and achievements.
- **Education Section**: A brief summary of my academic journey.
- **Contact Section**: A functional contact form powered by EmailJS.

---

## Features

- **Responsive Design**: Fully optimized for all devices.
- **Interactive Timeline**: Displays professional experiences and milestones.
- **Dynamic Contact Form**: Powered by EmailJS to allow users to reach out.
- **Customizable**: Easy to modify for future updates.
